APP ICON INSTRUCTIONS
=====================

You need to create 2 app icons and place them in the public folder:

1. icon-192.png (192x192 pixels)
2. icon-512.png (512x512 pixels)

EASY WAY TO CREATE ICONS:
1. Go to https://favicon.io/favicon-generator/
2. Use these settings:
   - Text: 💕 or M (for Molka)
   - Background: #ffb38e (peachy color)
   - Font: Rounded
3. Download and extract
4. Rename the largest icons to icon-192.png and icon-512.png
5. Place them in the public folder

OR use any image editor to create square PNG images with:
- A heart emoji 💕
- Or "M" letter
- Or a cat emoji 🐱
- Background color: #ffb38e

Once you add the icons, the app will be installable on phones!
